WDCC is back in semester 2 with our exciting "Hack Weekend"!
WDCC is teaming up with AUCS to provide a perfect opportunity for students of all skill levels to LEARN, CODE, and WIN in our "Hack Weekend". If you are passionate about solving real-world issues, you don't want to miss this one! For students who are not familiar with web development but wish to participate, we got you covered! You will get to learn how to build a React application from scratch using the MERN stack! (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js)
‍
Unlike traditional hackathons, we will also be running optional React workshops throughout the event. These workshops will be run by the Product Technical Lead at Voluntarily, Andrew Watkins, so you're in good hands. On top of all that, we are also bringing in professional engineers from the industry to mentor you throughout the hackathon.
