Club Activities

Website Projects

We provide opportunities for students to gain industry experience by establishing pro bono projects on website design and development.

Club Membership Systems

We will change the way clubs in UoA manage their memberships and payments through developing a new system, the new system will be faster and more efficient than the current ones that the clubs are using.

Web Development Workshop

Knowledge on web is arguably one of the most valuable skills in the tech industry. We host a range of workshops at different levels to teach members the skills they will need to participate in our projects.

Club schedule

We currently have three projects running within our club and our members gather together every week to work on these projects. These projects are Evento (an event registration system), CMLG (COVID-19 Multilingual Glossary) and Chancery (Investment Banking Interview Question Bank).

Through these projects, our members will complete tasks and get the opportunity to "develop and expand their professional knowledge." - Lucas Gao 2020
